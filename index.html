About

Born and raised in Chittagong Hill Tracts, I have been living here in Portugal since 2014. I have an undergraduate degree in economics from Monash university and a master degree in statistics from University of Évora.

Currently I am working as research fellow at University of Évora. I also teach part-time at Católica Lisbon School of Business & Economics.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing the guitar. I also enjoy spending time in RStudio Community and Statalist.

Fun Fact: My last name – Chakma – happens to be the name of the indigenous community that I belong to.
